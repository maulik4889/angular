What is a National Insurance Number (NIN) ?

Your NIN is a unique code that ensures your National Insurance Contributions and Tax are recorded against your name.

You need a NIN to get paid in the UK and receive payroll from your employer.


What you need to apply for a National Insurance Number

Right To Work

You need to have the legal right to work in the UK

to work here

Proof of Identity


You should be able to prove your identity. You can do so with one of these documents:
  • A passport from any country
  • A biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • A national identity card from an EU country , Norway, Liechtenstein or Switzerland

How to apply for a National Insurance Number (NIN)

  • You can apply online in the UK GOV website
  • It can take up to 4 weeks to receive your NIN
  • Be prepared to take photos of your passport and yourself during the online application
